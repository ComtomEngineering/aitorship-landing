The IP Crisis

AI Creates Incredible Content — But Who Owns It?

Generative AI presents unprecedented intellectual property challenges that threaten businesses and creators.

Training Data Liability

AI models trained on copyrighted material can produce outputs that resemble protected works, leading to expensive infringement claims and legal battles.

Ownership Vacuum

The U.S. Copyright Office refuses to register AI-generated works. Without human authorship documentation, your content has zero legal protection.

"Black Box" Problem

You have zero visibility into what copyrighted material influenced your AI outputs. This hidden liability can expose your business to massive, unforeseen risk.
